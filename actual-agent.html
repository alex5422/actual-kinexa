What Actual is.

Something is changing in the human being — biologically, cognitively, socially. Most thinking spaces are not looking at it directly.

Actual is a new space — not a magazine, not a consultancy, not a think tank in the traditional sense. It is an ecosystem built from clinical experience, scientific rigour, and the conviction that the most interesting thinking happens at the intersection of things that don't usually talk to each other: neuroscience and art history, AI and the body, medicine and philosophy.

The people behind Actual come from hospitals, operating rooms, high-level performance coaching, and research. Their names appear on the work, not on the institution. The institution is the work.

"The mind that has no leisure to compose itself is in an excessive state of slavery."

Who · The Kinexa ecosystem

Not one person.
A constellation.

Actual is the thinking space of the Kinexa ecosystem — a network built over thirty years at the intersection of clinical medicine, cognitive neuroscience, high-level performance, and now artificial intelligence.

The people behind the work come from AP-HP operating rooms, neurological rehabilitation wards, elite sports medicine, choreography, and music. They publish under their names. The institution stays in the background.

Actual is where this ecosystem thinks in public — slowly, honestly, without the usual protective packaging of institutional language.

What the ecosystem brings
→Thirty years of clinical neuroscience and physical medicine
→High-level performance coaching — athletes, musicians, executives
→Art history, Renaissance studies, the biomechanics of gesture
→Agentic AI and its effects on cognitive capital
→The conviction that truth requires a name — no anonymity here
